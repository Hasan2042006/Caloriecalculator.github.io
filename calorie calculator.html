Calorie Calculator

Calculate your daily calorie needs and macronutrient breakdown based on your goals.

How It Works

This calculator uses the Harris-Benedict Equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is the number of calories your body needs at rest. It then adjusts for your activity level and goal (weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain) to calculate your daily calorie needs.

The macronutrient breakdown is based on a 40:30:30 ratio (carbs:protein:fat), which is a general guideline for balanced nutrition. Adjustments can be made based on individual preferences or dietary requirements.

Disclaimer: This calculator provides general estimates and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Individual calorie and nutrient needs may vary based on factors such as metabolism, medical conditions, and lifestyle. Consult a healthcare provider or registered dietitian for personalized recommendations.
